ABOUT US

KNOWLEDGE IS POWER

A group of eminent and experienced people in the field of education for almost decade thought the need of this platform to support the needy Schools & Colleges. As we all know how badly the education system has been destroyed because of the pandemic (Covid-19) situation. As we are in a situation where we cannot continue the physical appearance of the education and we have to cope up with the online coaching as it is the current solution. We also know that we should not stop the continuity of the education of our children, so the myshikshanaonline.com is started. There are many Schools/Colleges suffering from providing the virtual education to the students in many places because of remote places, unaffordable of technical support, regular teachers finding difficult in using the current technology, etc., So our website is the best platform which provides the solutions to all the problems.
